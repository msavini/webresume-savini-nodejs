Synopsis

    Rainy Day Media was created to suggest songs, movies, tv shows that "matched" This was a 4-month long project completed in Web Science Systems Development at Rensselaer. This idea was implemented using NodeJS. The site utilized the Spotify API and TheMovieDB to bring information about options for digital entertainment that would compliment the current weather conditions experienced by users.

    The site was composed of 5 main pages: a home dashboard, a music page, a movie page, a television page, and a help/FAQ page. The design of the site was fairly simple to ensure the clear and concise presentation of data. Though it was not stylistically revolutionary, it was good practice working with RESTful web APIs.

    I was primarily influential with regard to the conceptual development and the front-end development of the site.
